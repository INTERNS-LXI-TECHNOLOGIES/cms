About Aset

Ahalia School of Engineering & Technology (ASET), Palakkad provides world-class technical education and training in the fields of Science, Engineering, Technology and Management to meritorious students from diverse socio-economic backgrounds. The college is located in a lush green campus to the beautiful view of the Western Ghats, and it provides a peaceful and congenial atmosphere, ideal for students for their overall holistic development. Ahalia School of Engineering and Technology offers five B.Tech. courses, that is complemented by various co-curriculur and extra-curriculur activities. ASET, managed by Ahalia International Foundation, is approved by AICTE, New Delhi and is affiliated to the APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University.
